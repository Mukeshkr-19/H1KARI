# Community Conduct

H1KARI contributors must communicate respectfully, critique work rather than
people, protect private information, and make space for different experience
levels and accessibility needs.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, deliberate exposure of
private information, or pressure to weaken safety and privacy controls are not
accepted in project spaces.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository maintainer's GitHub
contact or security-advisory channel when confidentiality is needed. Maintainers
may edit or remove contributions, restrict participation, or permanently ban
repeated or severe misconduct. Reports are handled as privately as practical and
without retaliation.
